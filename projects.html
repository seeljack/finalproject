My Projects

HealingBlue

I am in charge of developing Healing Blue, an innovative app designed to enhance access to mental health resources for faculty, staff, and students. My responsibilities included developing both the front-end, back-end, and data analytics components of the application, as well as collaborating closely with the design team. Currently, I am actively partnering with the Well-being Collective to bring this app to fruition, and I have the privilege of meeting with Santa Ono, the President of Michigan, to discuss our project.

GoDono

          My team and I designed the prototype for the GoDono app, leading to us raising $20,000 in funding. GoDono is LLC that connects users looking for community service to users looking to donate goods.
