My Projects

GoDono

         GoDono is LLC that connects users looking for community service to users looking to donate goods. Users looking to get community will pick up and drop off the items that need to be dropped off at the designated foodbank, shelter, etc. In exchange they recieve community service hours. As CTO I am in the process of designing and creating a functional app and website for GoDono. The ETA for the website is early January, and ETA for the app is Early Summer. They will both be posted here once finished

HealingBlue

Healing Blue is an app I am currently developing for the University of Michigan. Its purpose is to make mental health resources more accesable to students and faculty. By creating an app and having all these resources in an easy place to reach it will help students and faculty mentally.
