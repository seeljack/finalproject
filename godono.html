GoDono

A platform that bridges the gap between organizatoins/individuals seeking volunteers and those looking to volunteer in their communities.

Overview

GoDono empowers communities by bridging the gap between donors and volunteers. The app simplifies event coordination and drives social change through collaboration.

My Role

I contributed to the project by designing the app's user experience and helping secure $20,000 in funding to bring the idea to life.

Key Features

The app provides robust tools for volunteer management, features for users seeking volunteer opportunities, and a community rankings page, all designed to ensure accessibility and a seamless user experience for everyone.
